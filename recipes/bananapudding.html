Banana Pudding

Layered banana pudding in a serving dish

Description

This classic banana pudding is the ultimate crowd-pleaser, balancing creamy vanilla custard with crisp vanilla wafers and perfectly ripe sliced bananas. It’s the kind of nostalgic, soulful dessert that disappears as soon as it’s set out on the table. My husband absolutely loves it, often reaching for a second helping before the first is even finished! Whether you’re serving it at a backyard barbecue or a cozy family dinner, this recipe hits all the right notes of comfort and sweetness.

Ingredients

This recipe yields 20 servings
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 package (16 oz) vanilla wafers
  • 12 small bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Combine milk and pudding mix in bowl. Beat with whisk for 2 minutes.
  2. Fold in whipped topping.
  3. Lay repeating layers of wafers, bananas and pudding mixture until all ingredients are used.
  4. This deliciousness tastes best after chilling out for 1 hour. Top with crushed wafers when serving.
  5. Eat yo treat!
Nutrition Facts per serving
  • Calories: 329
  • Fat: 10 g
  • Carbs: 57 g
  • Protein: 4 g
